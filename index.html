Primary Inspirations

Chaos Magick

Chaos Magicians have popularized a method of sigil crafting based on the work of the artist Austin Osman Spare. In this widespread method, one takes a word or phrase and eliminates all of the vowels and duplicate letters. Then, one crafts the remaining letters into a cohesive symbol that becomes the sigil.

Sigil Witchery

Sigil Witchery, for lack of a better name, is the very personal method of creating sigils detailed by Laura Tempest Zakroff in her book of the same title. This practice involves cultivating a personal symbolic library, fully understanding the problem you are trying to tackle, breaking it down in to component parts, and then applying those symbols to a design intended to connect to those components.

Kamea Sigils

Kamea sigils make use of the seven planetary squares. Once you have removed the vowels and repeat letters from your word/phrase, you map the remaining based on alphabetical position to the square. After plotting the letters, you draw a small circle at the beginning, lines connecting the subsequent letters, and a small line at the final point. The resulting symbol is your sigil.
